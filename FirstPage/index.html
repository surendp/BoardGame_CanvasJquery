Game Play:


  • The game is divided in three different rows: first player area, void and second player area
  • The goal is to clear your area from all the cards.
  • Players take turn to press on their cards.
  • When a numbered card is pressed, the numbers are distributed one each on the other cards/spaces in anti-clockwise order
  • One count is lost every time the numbers have to pass through the ring, to get to opponent's area.
  • The game has lot of area for thinking and stratigizing
